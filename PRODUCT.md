# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Local clients in and around Buckingham who want reliable nail care, clear prices, and an easy way to call or find the salon before visiting.

## Product Purpose
DELUXE NAILS & SPA is a one-page salon website that helps visitors understand the services, check hours, see examples of finishes, and book by phone. Success means the visitor feels confident about quality, price, location, and cash-only payment before arriving.

## Brand Personality
Precise, calm, polished. The brand should feel clean and attentive rather than ornate, generic, or overly decorative.

## Anti-references
Avoid template salon pages with beige backgrounds, over-soft cards, stock-looking beauty poses, repeated uppercase labels, fake luxury language, and unclear pricing.

## Design Principles
- Show the care: use real-feeling service imagery and detailed finish shots.
- Make booking obvious: phone, address, hours, and payment information stay easy to find.
- Keep prices scannable: service categories should reduce overwhelm without hiding cost.
- Feel premium through restraint: clean spacing, crisp type, and one consistent accent color.
- Respect accessibility: strong contrast, visible focus states, readable type, and reduced-motion support.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation and gallery controls, reduced-motion alternatives, descriptive alt text, and no reliance on color alone for key information.
